Library

This is an ongoing list of material that has created a significant impact on how I see the world and operate within it.

The order is based on how strongly the concepts in them have changed how I think about life, design, and business.

That said, I have no gurus. I learn from these thinkers and their ideas but there is not a single person alive or dead whose every idea I blindly follow. I study all with equal amounts of criticism.

  1. Zero to One by Peter Thiel
  2. Siddhartha by Hermann Hesse
  3. Antifragile by Nassim Nicholas Taleb
  4. Are We Human? by Beatriz Colomina and Mark Wigley
  5. Cyrus The Great: The Arts of Leadership & War by Xenophon
  6. The Lessons of History by Will & Ariel Durant
  7. Thinking in Systems by Donella Meadows
  8. Competitive Advantage by Michael Porter
  9. Nichomachean Ethics by Aristotle
  10. Sagmeister & Walsh: Beauty
