About

Born in Manila. Grew up in Yonkers. Lover of design, technology, and the humanities.

Design is my life. I drew rockets, cellphones, houses, and sneakers when I was a child. I was lucky enough to attend a technical school at a young age to learn the fundamentals of electrical & mechanical engineering, computer science, and industrial drafting.

I moved to the U.S. for high school and applied all that creative energy into graphic & web design, theater, and poetry. I studied English & journalism in college and later combined my love for technology and the humanities to move into digital product design.

I've had the privilege to work on a lot of great products with great teams. Today, I constantly study and think about how product, design, and engineering can create better products, better teams, and better businesses that lead to better lives.
