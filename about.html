About

I love the craftmanship of design, the impact of strategy, the power of code & technology, and the leverage of leadership.

I'm passionate about designing beautiful digital products that empower people at scale, building new businesses that change the status quo, and cultivating high-performance teams.

I believe in the craft of design by honing and refining the fundamentals. I believe in being highly strategic and being as technologically adept as possible so we can be better builders and stewards of the things we are building. Most of all, I believe strong leadership and teamwork is what enables all of the above.

For more about me, you can see my past work or visit Feel Eternity, my design studio & consultancy.

Summary

Born in Manila, Philippines. Grew up in Yonkers, New York. Lover of design, technology, and the humanities.

Design is my life. I drew rockets, cellphones, houses, and sneakers when I was a child. Though I grew up modestly, my parents worked hard to be able to send me to a technical school to learn the fundamentals of electrical & mechanical engineering, computer science, and industrial drafting.

I moved to the U.S. for high school and applied all of that creative energy into graphic & web design, theater, and poetry. I studied English & journalism in college and later combined my love for technology and the humanities to move into digital product design.

I've had the privilege to work on a lot of great products with great teams. Today, I constantly study and think about how product, design, and engineering can create better products, better teams, and better businesses that lead to better lives.

In my free time, I scour the web for fonts and collect random visual inspiration to use in side projects. I like to get lost in a good book, watch a fun or deep TV series, and drink a good cocktail. I'm also a huge Yankees, Lakers, and Michigan Wolverines fan.

Purpose

Combining my life experience, personality, and personal gifts, I've arrived at three core purposes that I strive for:

  • Infuse technology with art and soul and do it through my work and lead by example.
  • Empower people to do things through technology in a manner that moves their being.
  • Inspire others—especially the youth and the underprivileged—to build technology with soul.

These things motivate me every single day, keep me grounded, and help me when things get difficult.

Ultimately, it's the standard by which I measure my life.
