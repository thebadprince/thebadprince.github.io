About

Born in Manila. Grew up in Yonkers. Lover of design, technology, and the humanities.

Design is my life. I drew rockets, cellphones, houses, and sneakers when I was a child. I was lucky enough to attend a technical school at a young age to learn the fundamentals of electrical & mechanical engineering, computer science, and industrial drafting.

I moved to the U.S. for high school and applied all that creative energy into graphic & web design, theater, and poetry. I studied English & journalism in college and later combined my love for technology and the humanities to move into digital product design.

I've had the privilege to work on a lot of great products with great teams. Today, I constantly study and think about how product, design, and engineering can create better products, better teams, and better businesses that lead to better lives.

Summary

I'm a product designer who loves the impact of strategy, the power of code, and the leverage of leadership. More than designing interfaces, I'm passionate about building new businesses and high-output teams.
  • I'm a strong proponent of continuous improvement not just for the products that we build but about us as individuals, as design teams, and as organizations. “Do not fight the last war” as Robert Greene would say.
  • I believe in honing the craft of design by practicing the fundamentals and learning new things together.
  • I believe in being a real champion for users by questioning assumptions and seeking real-world evidence.
  • I believe in getting better as technologists. Even if designers don't push production-level code, they should learn how their designs are built and where paradigms are shifting so they can be better partners to product & engineering, and truly be champions of their users.
  • I believe in clarity, collaboration, honesty, and integrity. The only way we'll do good work is by being good to each other, having rock-solid objectives that are agreed upon, similar values, tight feedback loops, and pushing for creativity.

For more about me, you can see my past work and how I consult for companies.
