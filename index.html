Jomi Cubol is a Product Strategist for Emerging Technologies.

I use design, product thinking, and engineering to build the software that powers tomorrow.

I study strategy, psychology, and philosophy as a framework for product development and how to think about the future.

Before that, I designed mobile apps and enterprise software for startups.

My mission is to spread individuality through technology to build a better future for humanity.
