Hi, my name is Jomi Cubol. I'm a designer obsessed with complex systems, mental models, and urban spaces. I’ve worked on consumer applications, enterprise software, widespread design systems, and digital media products.

I believe in the power of audacious missions, pure creativity as a competitive advantage, courage in spite of fear, relentlessly seeking process improvements, and making things better for the next generation.

I'm currently working at Condé Nast Co/Lab reimagining the future of media.
