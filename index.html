Intro

I'm intensely curious about design at scale — solving complex problems for wide audiences. I’ve worked on consumer applications, enterprise software, widespread design systems, and digital media products. Currently, I work at Condé Nast Co/Lab to reimagine the future of media.

I'm obsessed with the beauty of order, the challenge of chaos, and the power of form & structure. I believe in leveraging secrets, dissatisfaction, and amateurism to elevate the human condition.
