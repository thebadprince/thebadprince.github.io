About

I'm intensely curious about design at scale and designing for scale — how to find and address ways to empower, evoke, and energize wide audiences. I’ve designed and architected consumer applications, enterprise software, widespread design systems, and digital media products. Currently, I work at Condé Nast Co/Lab to reimagine the future of media.

I'm obsessed with the beauty of order, the challenge of chaos, and the power of form & structure. I value focus, efficiency, depth, mastery, and individuality above all things. I'm convinced strong missions, genuine trust, and collective creativity form a serious competitive advantage. I believe in leveraging secrets, dissatisfaction, and amateurism to elevate the human condition.
