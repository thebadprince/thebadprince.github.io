Hi, my name is Jomi Cubol. I'm a designer passionate about using systems thinking, strategy, and technology to solve complex problems. I’ve worked on consumer applications, enterprise software, widespread design systems, and digital media products. I'm currently working at Condé Nast Co/Lab to reimagine the future of media.

I'm obsessed with the beauty of order, the challenge of chaos, and the power of form & structure. I believe in leveraging secrets, living in dissatisfaction, and amateurism to elevate the human condition.
