Jomi Cubol is a designer, product strategist, and writer on a mission to empower humanity through design, technology, & philosophy.

I'm the Head of Design and Marketplace Product Lead at Photon Health, and the founder of Feel Eternity, a media brand & design consultancy for curent and aspiring design leaders.

Previously, I was Head of Product & Design for fitness startup Balanced, was Principal Design Architect & Group Design Manager for Thirty Madison, helped start the global design system for Condé Nast, and created new revenue products for its brands such as GQ, Architectural Digest, Golf Digest, and BRIDES.

Being a founding designer and building industry-transforming products has become my career expertise. Sharing what I learn and finding moments of depth and inspiration is my passion. For more info, learn more about me, read my blog, or email me for inquiries.
