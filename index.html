Previously, I was Head of Product & Design for fitness startup Balanced, was Principal Design Architect & Group Design Manager for Thirty Madison, helped start the global design system for Condé Nast and created new revenue products for GQ, Architectural Digest, Golf Digest, and BRIDES, and many others.

Being a founding designer and building industry-transforming products & teams has become my career expertise. Sharing what I learn and finding moments of depth and inspiration is my passion. For more info, learn more about me, see my past work, read my blog, or email me for inquiries.
