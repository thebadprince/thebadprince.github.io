What I'm Doing Now

My main objective and current side-project: learn how to fully build a product from strategy, to design, and all the way to implementation.

While I'm versed in the first two, forcing myself to work more in code and not just in paper and pixels helps me jump out of my comfort zone, work my vision faster, and become more fluent with the stack my designs are resting on. At the same time, building a product from scratch lets me dig deeper and explore new interface possibilities, extend my visual design skills, and widen my prototyping range. Most of all, it helps move closer to have the ability of a one-engineer startup. It's a tall-order, but if it wasn't then it wouldn't be worthy to be a challenge.

To help with this, I'm doing the Full-Stack Web Development track via Codecademy Pro and learning how to build a Swift app using Design+Code. I'll be sharing what I'm building more specifically when more solid pieces are in place.

Other than that, I'm trying to meditate and journal for 30 straight days (for starters). I'm also doing a modified powerbuilding program to build on my strength and retain muscle. For the meantime, my goal is to get back to form and comfortably deadlift 315lbs, squat 225 lbs, and bench 135 lbs on reps. Weight-wise I plan to get back down to 160 lbs. through a modified slow carb diet (no white carbs, no alcohol, but I am allowing some fruits and eggs).

I'll try to update this page with my progress every month from here on out.
