What I'm Doing Now

My main objective/current side-project: learn how to fully build a product from scratch, from strategy, to design, and all the way to implementation.

While I'm versed in the first two, forcing myself to work more in code and not just in paper and pixels helps me jump out of my comfort zone, work my vision faster, and become more fluent with the stack my designs are resting on. At the same time, building a product from scratch lets me dig deeper and explore new interface possibilities, extend my visual design skills, and widen my protoyping range.

To help with this, I'm doing the Full-Stack Web Development track via Codecademy Pro and learning how to build a Swift app using Design+Code.

Other than that, I'm trying to meditate and journal for 30 straight days (for starts). I'm also doing a modified powerbuilding program to build on my strength and retain muscle. My diet is on maintenance until I get the hang of my design-in-code objective. For the meantime, my goal is to get back to form and comfortably deadlift 315lbs, squat 225 lbs, and bench 135 lbs on reps.

I'll try to update this page with my progress every month from here on out.
