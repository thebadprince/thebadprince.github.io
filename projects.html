Condé Nast Design System

2018 – Present

A design system that seeks to power all of Condé Nast’s online properties: 20+ of the most iconic titles in media including The New Yorker, Vogue, GQ, Vanity Fair and others, serving 120M+ readers a month. Each of these websites have varying digital products and services carrying vastly different styles and identities. The goal is for each property to look and feel unique to its brand but work under one universal application.

This work is ongoing.

Design Partners: Consumer Design Team

Golf Digest Schools

2017

Golf Digest’s consumer revenue product, a premium video subscription service for golfers looking to improve their game. Users can watch highly-produced instructional material and gather actionable tips and exclusive training from the best instructors in the business. A “Netflix-meets-Coursera” for golfers of all levels.

Design Partners: Adrian Harwood & Sarah Rowe

2015 – 2016
