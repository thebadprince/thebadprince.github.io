Consultancy

I help software teams build the future through strong product vision, strategy, and design.

I’m a product development consultant experienced in product management, product design, business strategy, and operations. I’ve worked in various team sizes, product categories, business models, and technical stacks. I’ve taken many products—in both web and mobile, consumer and enterprise, ranging from zero to millions of users—from concept to validation to launch to scale.


The key is creating the right environment where great products can be built as a result of a strong vision combined with strong systems in place.

Outside of the most obvious things, what is right for one product or company is rarely right for another. If you’re just systematically copying what someone else is doing or how they did it, you cannot create any meaningful separation. This is why it’s crucial to develop your own strategy that is right for you and unique to your company, and a way of doing and thinking about things right for your stage and congruent with your values and mission.

You have to figure out what success means for your company, and what a great product would be that aligns with your version of success, and how to uniquely capture value in return.

This is where I come in: I can help formulate a strategy, define the right target metrics, translate it to a particular product vision down to the smallest details, and execute on it methodically—with precision & quality—with your team to reach your goals.

Process

Building the future is about doing the right things and doing things right. When you have both of these, you have flow.

How I accomplish this:

  1. I fully IMMERSE myself in the reality of your business, the stage of your organization, your company’s market, and your team’s specific challenges.
  2. I IMAGINE what a better future would be for your organization, figuring out the few most critical, highest leverage moves that can be made with the resources at your disposal, and what specific things your team can do to get there, with my assistance.
  3. I INTEGRATE myself fully into your current system and find crucial areas of improvement and start to shift things tactically to move towards the desired future state.
  4. I get INVOLVED in the day-to-day operations where necessary, rolling my sleeves and working directly with anyone I have to, from the CEO to the most junior person.
I will never claim to know your business better than you do. But with my help, you will find out some things you didn’t know about how your business currently runs and how to make it run much better.
